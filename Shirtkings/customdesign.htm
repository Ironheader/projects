Custom Designs? Made to order!

Is there an event you need a shirt made for? Want to spice up the workplace with shirts? We got you covered. We make custom designs and it is very easy to do. Just email us with your design and we can decide on a price and the amount of shirts produced, but we do have a few things you need to keep in mind, so we can make this go smoothly as possible.

The more complicated the design. The higher our intial price will be

More shirts you order, the longer it will take

Please order weeks in advance. Especially for events like family reunions, funerals, or mission trips

We want to get your shirts there in time. Unfortunate and unexpected events do happen. To lessen the effects of these we suggest you to order 2 to 3 weeks in advance

If we cannot fufill your order, you will get your refund

If we realize that you would not get your order in time due to us being busy. You will get your money back

If you decide to cancel the order while it is in production. You would get a 85% refund

We will keep 15% and you will get back 85%.It is a resource cost, and we will let you know when we will start production ahead of time. You have until that date to cancel to get a 100% of your money back.
